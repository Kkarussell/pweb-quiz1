Foods in Cilegon

Cilegon has a very delicious meals. First one is Rabeg, Rabeg is a savory-sweet slow simmered goat meat. This Rabeg usually served for aqiqah and weddings. Next one is Sate Bandeng or Bandeng Skewers. Just like the name of the dish, it's a usual skewer that made from bandeng fish. The unique part of this skewer is the making process. They took out the fish's meat and bones, separating from its skin without tearing the skin. After that fillet the fish, throw away the bones, grind the meat, season the meat, put the meat back inside the skin, then grill it just like a normal skewer, it's also very delicious.
