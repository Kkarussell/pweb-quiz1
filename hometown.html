My Hometown

Cilegon, a small city in Banten, the place where I was born, the place I called home. This city has its own superiority than any other cities here in Banten. Cilegon is the biggest industrial city in Banten, many well-known brand name that has their factory built here in Cilegon. The biggest one I can think is Krakatau Steel, we even got a nickname "Kota Baja" or in english "Steel City".
